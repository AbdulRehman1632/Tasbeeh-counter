"تسبیح"

بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ



Islamic prayer beads, also known as "tasbih" or "misbaha," are a significant spiritual tool used by Muslims to aid in the remembrance of Allah (God) and to engage in dhikr, which is the practice of repetitive utterances or reflections on the attributes of Allah.
